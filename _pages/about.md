I am Tingyu Zhang (张听雨), a Master's student in Computer Science at **Northeastern University Seattle** (4.0 GPA), with a Bachelor's in Applied Mathematics from **Southern University of Science and Technology**. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning research and engineering.

I published research on **reinforcement learning with entropy optimization** at [ICLR 2026](#) and co-authored an arXiv preprint on multi-armed bandits. My research interests include machine learning system development, reinforcement learning  and optimization fine tuning, GenAI and LLM-based agents.

I am currently based in the United States, open to research collaborations and industry opportunities in machine learning, perception, and autonomous systems.
